ABOUT SOFIA APARTMENTS

A Few About Us

Welcome to Sofia Apartments in Sarandë. Just minutes from Santa Quaranta, VIP, and Flamingo Beaches, our property offers a terrace with stunning views. Each unit includes air conditioning, free WiFi, private parking, a balcony, flat-screen TV, seating area, kitchen, and a bathroom with a bidet. Conveniently located near Butrint National Park and Ancient Fanoti, we're only 38 km from Corfu International Airport.
