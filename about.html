About Us

Welcome to Sofia Apartments in the heart of Sarandë. Our property offers comfortable accommodations just minutes from the beautiful Santa Quaranta Beach, VIP Beach, and Flamingo Beach.

Each unit includes air conditioning, free private parking, and free WiFi. Enjoy a private balcony, flat-screen TV, seating area, well-equipped kitchen, and private bathroom with a bidet. All units feature ample storage with spacious wardrobes. Located 15 km from Butrint National Park and 49 km from Ancient Fanoti, Sofia Apartments is perfect for both relaxation and exploration. The nearest airport is Corfu International Airport, 38 km away.
